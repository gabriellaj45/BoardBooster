Ways to Make Board Games Accessible


Here are the methods BoardBooster offers to add accessibility features to your board game:
Braille Labels
Add-on Braille labels that can be attached to the board, a card, or a game piece. This is useful for players who prefer to read Braille.
Tactile Markers
Physical markers to identify different regions of the game or different game pieces.
These markers can be combined to create a tactile overlay, which covers the entire board
