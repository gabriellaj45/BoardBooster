About Board Game Accessibility

This purpose of BoardBooster is to identify and address common accessibility problems faced when playing board games. Many different types of board game components are often inaccessible to blind players.

Some common problems are:
Instructions
Verifying that your game's instructions are available in an accessible physical or digital format
Game Board
Verifying that game board labels are readable and physical regions are defined
Game Pieces
Verifying that game pieces can be identified by touch
Cards
Verifying that cards have accessible labels
Dice or Spinners
Choosing an accessible method for generating random results
